### **Key Fields**
- **ID**: A unique identifier for the RFC (e.g., RFC-001).
- **Title**: The name of the RFC, linked to the full document.
- **Description**: A short summary of what the RFC covers.
- **Status**: Indicates whether the RFC is "Accepted," "Rejected," "Draft," or "Superseded."
- **Last Updated**: The date the RFC was last modified.

---

### **How to Use This Catalog**
1. **Find an RFC**: Use the table above to locate the RFC you need.
2. **View Details**: Click on the title to view the full document.
3. **Propose Changes**: To update or supersede an RFC, submit a new RFC or ADR.
